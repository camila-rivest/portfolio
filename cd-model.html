CD Case Model (WIP)

Blender

2026

For my first Blender project, due to its simple design, as well as my love for music and physical media, I decided to make a CD case. I’m very interested in learning more about 3D modeling. Once I become more proficient, I intend to integrate my models into After Effects to create more complex visuals. I’ve done many visuals in Photoshop, so adding movement and dimension to my pieces is the next step to elevating my practice.
