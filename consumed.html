Consumed

Bitsy

2024

Consumed is a short narrative game about power and how one can get lost in it and have their motives corrupted. After losing a battle and getting humiliated, the main character begins his search for ways to become stronger. However, during his quest for respect, he gets blinded by desire and ends up losing everything, including his humanity. This game was made using Bisty Color, a simple html game engine in a short amount of time.
