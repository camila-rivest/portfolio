Train Wreck is a projection mapping exercise combining footage depicting trains, animated to the beat of Eutow by Autechre. It was mapped onto a wall with tape marking the different zones.

Thanks to this project, I learned the process of defining the mapping area, as well as making sure the footage is well displayed.
